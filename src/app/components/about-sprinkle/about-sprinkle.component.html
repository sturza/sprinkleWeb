Sprinkle


Easily collect and manage the explosion of data from sensors, as in temperature and humidity. It helps you to take care of your garden when you are not home, by checking the data and sending comands back. Increases harvest by providing accurate data on the condition of each plant.
Each module is equipped with digital sensors that collect data and transmit it to Arduino. Arduino sends the data to an API that puts them in a database. From this database, the API is the one that extracts them, and processes them.
